Statistics Analysis

In medicine, the blood glucose exceeding 5mM/L implies having diabetes, and it exceeding 120mM/L implies being a severe patient. Finding the person whose glucose concentration is over 5mM/L or 120mM/L is our target.

0.1 vs 5 mM/L
0.1 vs 120 mM/L
0.1 model plot

First, we prove that there has difference between 0.1mM/L and 5mM/L (120mM/L) in the paired-difference T test part. And, we use the regression and prediction intervals to distinguish exceeding 120mM/L and 5mM/L from exceeding 0.1mM/L. From the result, 5mM/L can be distinguished from 0.1mM/L after 101 minutes, and 120mM/L can be distinguished from 0.1mM/L after 88 minutes.
