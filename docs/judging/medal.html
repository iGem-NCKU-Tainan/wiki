Silver

1. Validated Part / Validated Contribution

We experimentally validated our BioBrick device for Urine Glucose Detection (BBa_K1920001)and Safety((BBa_K1920006) . For our Detection (BBa_K1920001) BioBrick part , we characterized the relationship between glucose concentration and induced RFP fluorescent intensity .And for our Safety((BBa_K1920006) BioBrick part we measured the growth curve and performed a CFU assay.

3. Human Practices

In addition to perfecting our project and preventing diabetes , we held a variety of activities to connect U-know and iGEM to people (1) we launched videos via social media to provide health education to bust the common myth about diabetes and to provide correct information from doctors and specialists (2) we held synthetic biology workshops in our university NCKU to promote synthetic biology and interdisciplinary cooperation. for further information , please go to Human Practices

2. Proof of concept

To prove that U-know can prevent diabetes by detecting , we performed several Urine Functional Tests. Our Detection Circuit(BBa_K1920001) do differentiate Diabetic urine sample with Control , and the sensitivity is 95% , which was already further improved to 100% For Safety , we tested our Safety Circuit(BBa_K1920006) and proved that our design do protect U-know users as well as the environment by killing bacteria.

To know more , please look at Result/Proof
