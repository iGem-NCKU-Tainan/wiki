JUDGING / Safety
Method
How we are addressing any safety issues in our project?
  • Choosing a non-pathogenic chassis: E. coli DH5α, E. coli BL21 (DE3)
  • In our product, the chassis is in small enclosed space as a barrier to prevent contamination.
  • In the enclosed space, there are “suicide-gene” inducers to kill E. coli in the bio-sensing strip.
Safety plan

Our E. coli will sensor urea glucose. When the glucose level is high, the bacteria can express red fluorescent protein (RFP) as reporter to give an signal. The signal from fluorescence can be detected using excitation. After the processing, the “suicide-gene” inducers, arabinose, will induce the pBAD promoter (BBa_K206000) and then activate the transcription of Lysis protein (BBa_K117000).
